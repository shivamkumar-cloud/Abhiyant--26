About NCC & Our Unit

The Pride of the Nation, The Future of Our College

The National Cadet Corps

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is the youth wing of the Indian Armed Forces with its headquarters in New Delhi, India. It is open to school and college students on a voluntary basis.

Founded under the National Cadet Corps Act of 1948, the NCC motto is "Unity and Discipline". Its aim is to develop character, comradeship, discipline, a secular outlook, the spirit of adventure, and ideals of selfless service amongst young citizens.

NCC at Pusa Institute of Technology

Our college NCC unit stands as a beacon of discipline and excellence. We train cadets in drill, weapon handling, map reading, leadership, and social service. Our cadets have consistently brought laurels to the institute in various camps and competitions.

The unit is guided by the principle of "Nation First, Always First", preparing responsible citizens and future leaders for our country.
